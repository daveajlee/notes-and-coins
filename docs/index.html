Simple app to manage the amount of money in your physical wallet!

The Notes & Coins App helps you to maintain an overview of the amount of money you have in your physical wallet. In addition to storing and retrieving the amount of money that you have in your physical wallet, a notification will be shown if you get below a defined limit so you have time to go to the bank to get more cash. An overview with charts and a full history of transactions gives you an overview of what you have used cash for in the last few days, weeks or months. Download the "Notes & Coins" App and never lose the overview of the cash in your wallet again!
